Your hemoglobin of 10.2 g/dL is below the normal range (13.5–17.5 g/dL for adult men, 12–15.5 g/dL for women). This indicates mild anemia.

What this means: You may feel fatigue, shortness of breath, or look pale.

Recommended actions:
• Consult a Hematologist or General Physician
• Increase iron-rich foods (spinach, lentils, red meat)
• Consider iron supplement (after doctor advice)
